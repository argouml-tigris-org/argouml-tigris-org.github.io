Linus Tolke

Joined the project in 2001 and became project leader in the summer of 2002.

Was a mentor when ArgoUML took part in the Google Summer of Code 2006 and 2007. In 2006 the mentor for the ArgoPrint work done by Ion Savin. In 2007 the mentor for the Profiles work done by Marcos Aurélio.

Personal life

Born and raised in Svedala outside Malmö in southern Sweden.

Came in real contact with computers and software development while studying at the Linköping University in Linköping, Sweden.

Employed at Alten computer consultant company in Stockholm working mostly for customers in the Stockholm area. Living in Tumba,_Sweden just south of Stockholm.
