Christian López Espínola <penyaskito>

In 2006, he started being involved in ArgoUML module development.

In the summer of 2007, he applied for ArgoUML for working on a new module that would provide a new implementation of sequence diagrams, mentored by Bob Tarling.

After that summer, he became a committer of the ArgoUML project.

In 2008, he applied again for providing a new implementation of property panels, based on information generated semi-automatically from the UML metamodel. This work was mentored by Bob Tarling.

Personal life

Born in Barcelona, Spain on the 27th August, 1985, now living in Sevilla.

Came in real contact with computers and software development while studying at Universidad de Sevilla, Spain.
